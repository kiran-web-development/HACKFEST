## The Problem & Solution (0:30 - 1:00)
"Current solutions are fragmented. You have inventory apps, donation apps, and waste trackers, but nothing connects them. 

EcoPantry unifies the entire lifecycle:
1.  **Smart Pantry**: We track inventory specifically to prevent expiry.
2.  **Waste Logging**: When food *is* tossed, we track the financial and carbon impact, making the invisible cost visible.
3.  **Action**: Most importantly, we act. Before food expires, our marketplace connects donors with local communities instantly."
